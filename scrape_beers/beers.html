Beer Manufacturers/Microbrewers and Brands List

Report date: November 12, 2013

The ministry compiles a list of Ontario beer manufacturers that are considered microbrewers for the sales year ending March 2, 2014.

NOTE: Purchases from the Liquor Control Board of Ontario or from a person who previously purchased the beer from the Liquor Control Board of Ontario are not subject to the beer tax.

This list will enable consumers to identify brands of beer made by microbrewers that are subject to the beer tax rates applicable to beer made by microbrewers. If the brand of beer purchased is on the list, consumers can then use the Beer Tax Information Sheet to calculate the beer tax payable on the beer by multiplying the tax rate that applies to the beer by the volume of the product purchased.
